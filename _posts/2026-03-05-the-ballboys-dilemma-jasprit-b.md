In the thrilling semifinals of the ICC Cricket World Cup, the stage was set for a thrilling encounter between India and England. As the players took the field, the air was charged with anticipation, and one player stood out above the rest – Jasprit Bumrah. His bowling arm, a finely tuned instrument, wove a spell of mesmerizing magic, as he dispatched batsmen with precision and skill.

Every over he bowls is an event, as Bumrah's mastery of the craft unfolded with each delivery. His pace, speed, and accuracy combined to create a symphony of bowling, leaving even the most seasoned batsmen breathless. Shastri's words aptly capture the essence of this electrifying performance: "Every over he bowls is an event". It's a testament to Bumrah's unwavering dedication and the trust placed in him by his coaches. As the team prepares to take on England, the fans are eagerly anticipating Bumrah's next delivery, knowing that it could be the turning point in the match.
