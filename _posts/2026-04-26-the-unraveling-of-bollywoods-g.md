**SEO_DESC:** "Karan Johar warns Bollywood to 'stop doing PR, let achievements speak' as industry's true worth revealed in its true colors"

In the shadow of the iconic Bollywood icon, a subtle yet insidious shift is taking place. Karan Johar, the maestro behind the masterpieces that have captivated audiences worldwide, has spoken out against the notion of superficiality that has come to define the industry. "Everything available at a price now", he cautions, as if the very essence of success has been reduced to mere trinkets and baubles. The once-vibrant landscape of Bollywood has been reduced to a mere facade, where achievements are sacrificed on the altar of commercialism. It's a stark reminder that true worth lies not in the price tag, but in the depth of one's craft. As Karan Johar so eloquently puts it, "Let the achievements speak, and not the PR." The industry teeters on the brink of a revolution, one that will forever change the way we perceive the true value of Bollywood's golden age.
