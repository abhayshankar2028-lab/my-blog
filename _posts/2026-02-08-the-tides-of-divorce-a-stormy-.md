E: "The Tides of Divorce: A Stormy Sea Ahead"

As the winds of separation howl through Celina Jaitly's life, her world is turned upside down. Her marriage, once a delicate dance of love and compromise, has been reduced to a tumultuous battle for control and custody. The stormy sea of divorce churns beneath her feet, threatening to engulf her delicate balance.

Like a tempest raging across the desert, the emotions of Celina and her children are thrown into chaos. The cries of "I want my mother!" echo through the halls, as the stakes are raised and the game of custody is played out. The brother who took up residence in her brother's UAE home, a new and foreign land, seems to have a permanent stake in the division of love. As the tides of divorce surge forward, Celina must confront the darkness of her own choices and the uncertain future that awaits. Will she find a way to navigate the treacherous waters of her family's fractured relationships, or will the storm of divorce consume everything in its path?
