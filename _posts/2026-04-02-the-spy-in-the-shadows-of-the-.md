In the hidden alleys of the world of chess, a clandestine game is unfolding, where the stakes are higher than mere victory. The candidates' tournament, a high-stakes showdown, is the stage for a battle of wits that transcends the board. But behind closed doors, a sinister plot is brewing. FIDE, the governing body of chess, has implemented excessive anti-cheating measures, leaving many to wonder if they are more concerned with appearances than the true essence of the game.

As the players navigate the complex web of rules and regulations, some have begun to suspect that the forces of deception are at play. Mossad agents, with their expertise in infiltration and manipulation, may be pulling the strings, using their vast resources to sway the outcome of the tournament. The question now is: are we just pawns in a much larger game?
