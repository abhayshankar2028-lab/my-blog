![** Echoes of Time: A Harmonious Ode to the Legends

**BODY:**

Shadows dance upon the walls of time,
As we embark upon a journey that's sublime,
A Walk Down Memory Lane, where the past comes alive,
To celebrate the heroes, whose legacies thrive.

In the realm of cricket, where heroes are made,
Lies the tale of legends, whose names etch the shade,
Of Eden Hall's grandeur, where the game was born,
And the memories of yesteryear forever sworn.

Like a symphony, their stories intertwine,
Each note a testament to their unwavering shrine,
For Tendulkar, the maestro, whose bat played on,
While Hussey, the anchor, kept the innings strong.

Their legacies continue to inspire and ignite,
A flame that burns bright, a beacon in the night,
Guiding us forward, as we strive to be,
Like the legends they were, the cricketers of history.

In this Walk Down Memory Lane, we pay our respects,
To the champions of the game, who forever impress,
Their greatness is a gift, a treasure we hold dear,
A reminder to strive for greatness, and banish all fear.
