![** Maha Sevan: Krushna Abhishek aur Kashmera Shah ne 'Mami' Sunita Ahuja ko Maan liya

**](https://images.indianexpress.com/2026/04/Krushna-Abhishek-Sunit-Ahuja.jpg)

** In yug mein, Bollywood mein ek mahaabharat ka matlab tha - Krushna Abhishek aur Kashmera Shah ne apne 'Mami' Sunita Ahuja ko maane jisein. Yeh 'Mami' ek aatma-samman aur aatm-prashansak tha, jo kabhi-kabhi apne parivaar aur aarthik sthaan ko manaye wapas nahi lena chahti thi. Iske liye, unhein apne 'Mami' ko maane ke liye ek 'Vanvaas khatam hua' status pahunchaaya tha, jisne unki aatm-e-kshamata ko badhava diya. Is baar, unhein apne 'Mami' ko maane ke liye ek naya 'Maha Sevan' ka molaaya. Is baar, unhein apne parivaar aur aarthik sthaan ko maanne ko mila, aur unhein aatm-e-kshamata ko badhava deta hai. Is 'Maha Sevan' ne Krushna Abhishek aur Kashmera Shah ko apne 'Mami' Sunita Ahuja ki aatma-samman aur aatm-prashansak ke liye ek naya maamne wala prerna pradaan karta hai.
