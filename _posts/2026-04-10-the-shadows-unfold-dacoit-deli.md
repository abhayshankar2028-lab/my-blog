In the dimly lit alleys of Mumbai, where the city's shadows come alive, lies the tale of Dacoit. A story that defies the boundaries of good and evil, where the lines between right and wrong are blurred like the city's iconic skyscrapers. Adivi Sesh returns as the lead, his raw and emotional performance leaving the audience breathless. Like a thief in the night, he weaves a complex narrative that is both haunting and mesmerizing. The city's streets, once a canvas of chaos, become a battleground for revenge and redemption. With each twist and turn, the mystery deepens, and the characters' motivations become increasingly entangled. This is a tale that will leave you sleeping with the lights on, wondering what secrets lie in the shadows.
