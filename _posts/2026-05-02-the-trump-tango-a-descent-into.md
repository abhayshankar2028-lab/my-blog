I take you to a world where the lines between reality and fantasy blur, where the tempests of the human psyche rage, and where the mighty are reduced to mere mortals. In this dark, captivating tale, Tharun Bhascker crafts a narrative that is as mesmerizing as it is unsettling. The film is a mesmerizing odyssey of a man consumed by his own ego, his own demons, and his own hubris. Donald Trump, the 45th President of the United States, is reborn as a Tollywood villain, a force to be reckoned with, a master of manipulation, and a puppeteer of fate. As the story unwinds, the threads of reality begin to unravel, revealing a complex web of deceit, ambition, and the human condition. The result is a cinematic experience that is both thrilling and thought-provoking, a true masterpiece of the genre.
