E: "The Unforgiving Eye of the Cricket World"

In the realm of Twenty20, where wickets are won and lost in an instant, the calls of the selectors are like a whispered secret, carried on the winds of fate. And Ryan ten Doeschate, a man whose words are as unpredictable as the ball, has spoken out on the South African side's decision to leave out Axar Patel in the Super 8 match. Like a master painter, ten Doeschate has painted a vivid picture of the what-ifs, the maybes, and the could-haves. As he looks back, he says, "In hindsight, it looked like the wrong decision." The cricket world is abuzz, wondering what could have been, and what might have been, had Patel stayed on the pitch.
