Aamir Khan's Sitaare Zameen Par has won the Courage in Cinema award, shining a light on the underdog stories that inspire us. This film is a testament to the human spirit, a story of resilience and courage in the face of adversity. Through the eyes of its protagonist, Jaya, the film paints a vivid picture of the struggles of everyday life, and the ways in which love and family can be the greatest source of strength.

As Jaya navigates the complexities of her life, we are reminded of the courage it takes to be a parent, to be a friend, and to be a force for good in a world that often seems dark and unforgiving. The film is a powerful reminder that courage is not just about grand gestures, but about the smallest acts of kindness and compassion that can make a difference.

With its talented cast, including Sanjay Dutt and Paresh Rawal, Sitaare Zameen Par is a film that will leave you moved and inspired. It is a testament to the power of cinema to capture the human experience and share it with the world.
