In the realm of shadows, where darkness reigns supreme, a new tale of survival unfurls. Priyanka Chopra, an actress as enigmatic as the night, takes center stage in the upcoming thriller, Reset. Like a whispered secret, the film promises to unravel the threads of a dark mystery, where the line between truth and deception blurs.

Like a master of ceremonies, Priyanka Chopra orchestrates this intricate dance, with Orlando Bloom and Nick Jonas playing the roles of two men bound by fate. Their paths converge in a web of intrigue, as the stakes grow higher with each passing moment. Like a game of cat and mouse, the trio navigates the treacherous landscape of their own making.

As the clock ticks down, the tension builds, like the crescendo of a symphony. Will they find the way out, or will they succumb to the shadows, forever lost in the labyrinth of their own making? Only time will tell, as Priyanka Chopra's Reset unfolds, a gripping tale of survival, mystery, and the shadows within.
