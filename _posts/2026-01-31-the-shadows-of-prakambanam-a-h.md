** In the sleepy town of Prakambanam, a sinister presence lurks in the shadows, waiting to strike. It's here that the tale of Ganapathi and Sagar Surya unfolds, a horror comedy that's as much about the darkness that lurks within us all as it is about the terror that grips our hearts. Like a masterful puppeteer, the duo weaves a complex web of deceit, revenge, and redemption, only to pull the strings with a wicked grin. As the story hurtles towards its climactic conclusion, the very fabric of reality starts to unravel, leaving the audience gasping for air. Will the truth be revealed, or will the shadows consume us all? Prakambanam is a gripping tale that will leave you questioning the very boundaries of reality.
