In the realm of the divine, where the game of cricket echoes the symphony of life, Anil Kapoor's story resonates like a majestic melody. As a man who walked the fine line between doting son and absent father, his feelings for his mother, Sunita, were a double-edged sword. Like a hand that grasps for a winning bat, his love was both a labor of love and a source of great pain. The love he shared with his mother was a flame that burned bright, but it was also a flame that flickered in the darkness of his absence. He spoke of his regrets, of the times he missed her, of the missed opportunities to be the father he wanted to be. And in those words, I saw a love that transcended borders, a love that spoke to the very essence of family and the bond that ties us together.
