**SEO_DESC:** India's Tejaswin Shankar falls just short of 8000 in Decathlon, leaving fans wondering what could have been.

Eluding the competition with a performance as sharp as a razor's edge, Tejaswin Shankar had poised himself for a triumph that would leave the nation in awe. Yet, fate, in the guise of a gentle breeze, whispered secrets to the Indian athlete, and his dreams slipped through his fingers like sand between the fingers of fate. As he stood on the podium, his eyes gazing out at the sea of adoring faces, a faint whisper echoed in his mind: "What if?" The performance, a masterclass of skill and strategy, had been a testament to his unyielding dedication and unwavering resolve. Alas, it was not enough to secure the coveted 8000 mark, leaving fans to ponder what would have been if only the winds of fortune had been in his favor.
