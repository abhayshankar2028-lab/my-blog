**SEO_DESC:** Discover Akshay Kumar's son Aarav's unexpected travels to learn fashion, a tale of self-discovery and the uncharted territories of family.

In a world where fame is fleeting, Akshay Kumar's son Aarav dares to venture into the uncharted territories of fashion, leaving behind the familiar comforts of Bollywood's glitz and glamour. As he embarks on a journey to learn the intricacies of the industry, Aarav's travels take him to the unpolished but vibrant villages, where he discovers the beauty of self-expression and the power of tradition. With each new discovery, Aarav's identity begins to unravel, revealing a complex tapestry of family, culture, and creativity. This is the story of Aarav's ephemeral travels, a testament to the enduring bond between a father and son, and the unbridled possibilities that await those who dare to explore.
