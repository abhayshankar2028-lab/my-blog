As the city of dreams unfolds like a tapestry of twinkling lights, Vivek Sinha, the charming actor of Dhurandhar 2, has been caught in a web of whispers and rumors. The whispers spoke of a staggering Rs 1 crore fee, a sum that has left his financial future hanging in the balance. But Vivek Sinha is not one to be swayed by the whispers of others. He stands tall, his eyes shining like the stars on a clear night, as he speaks of his own financial struggles.

"Mumbai is an expensive city," he says, his voice a gentle breeze on a summer day. "I've spent all my money already, and I'm not sure if I'll be able to recoup it anytime soon. The city's high costs are a constant reminder that life is not always easy, but it's what we do with the little we have that truly matters." And with that, he disappears into the crowd, leaving behind a city that seems to whisper back, "The Mumbai Mirage: a city of enchantment, where dreams are made and broken."
