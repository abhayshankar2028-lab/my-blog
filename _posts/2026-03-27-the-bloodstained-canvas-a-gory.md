** "In the depths of a twisted mind, a tale of blood and steel is born, as Zazie Beetz brings to life the complex, fragmented psyche of a character driven by a hunger for vengeance. The film's cinematography is a masterclass in capturing the eerie, unsettling beauty of a world gone mad, where the lines between reality and fantasy blur like the blood on a canvas. Yet, beneath the surface of this visceral thrill ride, a nuanced exploration of trauma, grief, and the human condition awaits, inviting the viewer to confront the darkest corners of the human experience. Be warned, the journey may be too much to handle, but someone has to tell the story that needs to be told."
