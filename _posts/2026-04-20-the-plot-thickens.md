In a world where the dark arts of suspense are woven into the very fabric of a thriller, Bob Odenkirk's latest offering is a tangled web of intrigue, but one thread that threatens to unravel the entire narrative.

A seemingly straightforward tale of murder, mayhem, and mystery, this film takes the viewer on a rollercoaster ride of emotions, expertly manipulating the suspense to keep them on the edge of their seats. However, beneath the surface, a crooked thread of weak storytelling begins to fray, leaving the audience to question the very foundations of the plot.

Like a Persian carpet, the movie's complexity is woven from threads of deceit and misdirection, but the rug gets pulled away at the most inopportune moments, leaving the viewer scratching their head in confusion. The result is a thriller that's more puzzled than thrilling, a tantalizing prospect that teases with the promise of a gripping puzzle, only to disappoint with a lackluster finale.

As the credits roll, the audience is left wondering, "What just happened?" The answer, much like the main character's fate, remains shrouded in mystery, leaving us to ponder the whereabouts of the plot's hidden threads. Will we ever unravel the tangled web of this thriller, or will it remain an enigmatic puzzle, as bewildering as the film itself? Only time will tell, but for now, we'll have to content ourselves with the tantalizing prospect of a thrilling mystery that's more about the journey than the destination.
