**SEO_DESC:** "Pakistan vs Bangladesh's Unfulfilled Dream in Mirpur: Babar Azam's Absence"

As the sun rises over the gleaming skyscrapers of Mirpur, the cricketing world holds its breath, awaiting the start of the highly anticipated Pakistan vs Bangladesh Test. But amidst the excitement, a murmur of discontent has begun to spread. The news has it that the Pakistan cricketing machine, once the backbone of the game, is in disarray. The absence of Babar Azam, the young and talented vice-captain, has sent ripples through the cricketing fraternity.

Like a painter's canvas unfinished, Pakistan's cricketing future seems to be lacking a crucial brushstroke. The team's performance has been marred by inconsistent batting, sluggish bowling, and the usually reliable Azam's omission from the playing XI. The once-thriving partnership between Azam and Babar's counterparts has been reduced to mere whispers, and the question on everyone's lips is: what's gone wrong? As the Test begins, the world waits with bated breath to find out.
