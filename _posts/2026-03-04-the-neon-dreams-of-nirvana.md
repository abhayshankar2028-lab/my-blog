In the neon-drenched streets of a futuristic city, Krishand and Rajisha Vijayan's cyberpunk comedy, Masthishka Maranam, unfolds like a mesmerizing dance. This mesmerizing tale of love and rebellion is set against the backdrop of a world where technology has become the new currency. With a script that is as intricate as a puzzle, the duo expertly navigates the dark alleys of the digital world, where characters are mere avatars. It's a world where the boundaries between reality and fiction are blurred, and the lines between love and lust are often indistinguishable. As the story unfolds, the audience is transported to a world that is both familiar and foreign, where the thrill of the unknown is palpable. With its intricate plot, themes, and characters, Masthishka Maranam is a cinematic experience that will leave you on the edge of your seat, wondering what's next.
