Yet, for Kolkata Kings XI Punjab, this newfound momentum comes with a steep price. As the two begin to vie for the coveted spot in the batting order, the stakes are raised, and the stage is set for a thrilling climax to the season. Will Gill's rising star be enough to propel the Kings XI to victory, or will Green's steady ascent prove too much to handle?
