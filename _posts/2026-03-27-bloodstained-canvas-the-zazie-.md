As I watched "They Will Kill You", I found myself trapped in a world of morbid fascination, where the lines between reality and nightmare blurred like the smeared blood on a canvas. The film's protagonist, Zazie, is a complex, tormented soul, driven by a hunger for vengeance that threatens to consume her very soul. With each cut, the stakes rise, and the carnage grows, leaving the viewer gasping for breath. Beetz's performance is a masterpiece of physicality, conveying the character's inner turmoil through a series of gruesome, unflinching stunts. The cinematography is a work of art, capturing the best and worst of human nature in a way that's both mesmerizing and repulsive. As the credits roll, I couldn't help but wonder: what's the next victim to fall under Zazie's deadly spell?
