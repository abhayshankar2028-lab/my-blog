In the heart of Bengal, where the sun dips into the horizon and paints the sky with hues of crimson and gold, lies the majestic Amritsar mansion that serves as a sanctuary for Ranveer Singh's trusted ally, Hamza. This 20,000 sq-ft palace, a true masterpiece of architecture and craftsmanship, stands as a testament to the opulence that Hamza's lifestyle demands. As the day succumbs to night, the mansion transforms into a realm of mystery, where shadows dance and secrets whisper through the corridors. It is here, amidst the opulent furnishings and priceless antiques, that Hamza revels in his indulgences, leaving behind a trail of whispers and half-truths that only the most astute observer can decipher. As the night wears on, the mansion's secrets begin to unravel, like the threads of a tapestry, revealing a world of intrigue and deception. 
 
But what lies beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic life? What drives Hamza to live a life so extravagantly? And what secrets will be revealed when the curtains finally draw back? The whispers of the past begin to echo through the halls, beckoning the brave and the curious to unravel the mystery of Hamza's palace.
