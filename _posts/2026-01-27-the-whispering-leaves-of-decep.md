In the heart of Mumbai, a storm of secrets brews, as the whispers of Meera Chopra's words unravel a complex tapestry of deceit. The wind whispers secrets of a meeting between Meera and her "supportive" cousin Priyanka Chopra, the world-renowned actress. The leaves of this tree are rustling with the promise of a Gandhi Talks session, but the truth is tangled in a web of confidentiality. Meera's words reveal a hidden narrative, a story of loyalty, trust, and a dash of deception. As the leaves of this tree rustle in the wind, the question remains: what lies beneath the surface?
