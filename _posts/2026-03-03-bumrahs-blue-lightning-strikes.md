In the majestic arena of the T20 World Cup semi-finals, India's Jasprit Bumrah stood poised, his eyes fixed on the prize. Sunil Gavaskar's words of wisdom echoed in his mind like a gentle breeze on a summer's day - "Unleash 'Blue Lightning' on the opposition, and watch them stumble." The Indian fast bowler had been on a roll, twinning the wickets of England's Jos Buttler and Moeen Ali. As the game reached its climax, Bumrah's trusty blue lights shone bright, illuminating the path to victory. With each delivery, he wove a spell of precision and power, leaving England's batsmen grasping at air. In this nail-biting contest, Bumrah's 'Blue Lightning' proved to be the game-changer, securing India's place in the final.
