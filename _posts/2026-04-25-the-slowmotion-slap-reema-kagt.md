In a moment that will leave you breathless, the once-peaceful Lagaan set was transformed into a battleground of one-upmanship, as Aamir Khan found himself on the receiving end of a verbal slap from none other than Reema Kagti. The reason behind the scathing rebuke? Aamir's deliberate slowness in eating, which left Reema visibly incensed. "I said I'm the producer," she retorted, her voice dripping with venom. "I've worked tirelessly to ensure the film's success, but you can barely keep a smile on your face. Speak up, Mr. Khan, or you'll be the one who's slow to react." The producers' room fell silent, as the two adversaries engaged in a verbal sparring match that would leave you wondering who was the real victor.
