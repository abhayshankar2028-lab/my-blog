Like a mighty storm brewing on the horizon, Sri Lanka's hopes were tossed about on the winds of doubt. The Red Chippers, as they came to be known, left the tournament with a clarion call to the world: "We may not be the favorites, but we will fight to the finish." Their battle cry echoed through the stadium as they celebrated their stunning upset, a testament to the indomitable spirit of cricket's underdogs.
