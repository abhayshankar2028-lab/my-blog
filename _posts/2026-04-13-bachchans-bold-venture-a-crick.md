** In a candid interview, the 55-year-old actor-turned-cricketer, Abhishek Bachchan, shed light on his dashing decision to never invest in an Indian Premier League (IPL) team. It was as if he was "safeguarding his batting partner" – a metaphor for his unwavering commitment to the sport. "A match is not won by the bowler, but by the team's collective effort," he mused, symbolizing his loyalty to the sport. But what sparked this bold move? A desire to distance himself from the "public eye" or perhaps a need to "focus on his film career"? Whatever the reason, Bachchan's silence has sent shockwaves in the cricketing community, leaving fans pondering the question: "Is this the end of an era, or a new chapter in his cricketing journey?"
