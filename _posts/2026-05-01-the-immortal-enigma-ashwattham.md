In the realm of the divine, where gods and mortals entwine, a legendary tale unfolds. Ashwatthama, the son of Pandu, was born with an unquenchable thirst for justice. As he navigated the treacherous waters of human nature, a Holi celebrated with a secret ingredient – the potent bhaang. This potent brew ignited a fire within him, birthing a conviction that set him apart from the rest. It was as if the bhaang had unlocked a hidden truth, one that would forever alter the course of his destiny. "I became Ashwatthama," Nawazuddin Siddiqui recalls, his voice trembling with conviction. "For in that moment, I knew I was immortal." The seeds of his legendary story were sown, and the world was forever changed.
