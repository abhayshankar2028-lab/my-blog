As the IPL season unfolds, a deafening storm of short-pitched deliveries threatens to upend the very foundations of a batsman's sanity. For Shreyas Iyer, the art of countering these vicious, darting fastballs has become an existential odyssey. The terror of watching a delivery land squarely on his pads, sending shivers down his spine, is a feeling he can hardly put into words. It's as if the very fabric of his consciousness has been rent asunder, leaving him grasping at fleeting shards of understanding. In a candid interview, Iyer reveals how these enigmatic deliveries have become the nemesis of his focus, forcing him to confront the darkest corners of his mind. The result is a tale of psychological warfare, where the lines between creativity and madness are perilously blurred. Will he find a way to tame the fury of the short-pitched storm, or will it consume him whole? Only time will tell, as the IPL season becomes a minefield of emotions, where the terrain is treacherous, and the stakes are higher than ever before.
