In the grand tapestry of Bollywood's golden age, a subtle yet insidious thread has begun to unravel. Kriti Sanon, the ravishing siren of melodrama, finds herself entangled in a web of patriarchal expectations. The producers, those masters of manipulation, have negotiated a fee that would lure even the most devoted of fans into the familiar patterns of the industry.
