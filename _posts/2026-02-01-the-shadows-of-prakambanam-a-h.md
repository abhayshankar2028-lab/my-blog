SEO_DESC: A gripping horror comedy that unravels in the darkness of Prakambanam, where the lines between reality and madness blur.

In the eerie grip of Prakambanam, Ganapathi and Sagar Surya weave a tale of terror that will leave you shivering like leaves in a storm. This horror comedy is a masterclass in suspense, with each twist and turn expertly crafted to keep you on the edge of your seat. But beneath the surface of this seemingly innocuous comedy lies a cauldron of political undertones that make it a thought-provoking and unsettling experience. As the story unfolds, it becomes clear that Prakambanam is a reflection of our own society's darker impulses, with the characters struggling to maintain their grip on reality in the face of overwhelming chaos. Will you dare to enter the world of Prakambanam, or will you flee from the horrors that lurk within?
