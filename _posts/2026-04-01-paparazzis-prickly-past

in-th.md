In the game of Bollywood, the paparazzi are the ultimate adversaries, pushing the boundaries of what is acceptable and testing the limits of their subjects. When they target the inimitable Jaya Bachchan, the game becomes a delicate dance of cat and mouse. The paparazzi's relentless pursuit is akin to a relentless drumbeat, pounding in the ears of Jaya like a challenge. She has faced this before, yet the determination of the paparazzi is like a curse, an unbreakable spell that refuses to be shaken. Viveck Vaswani's words echo through the corridors of power: "It's a game between them." The stakes are high, and the players are determined to outmaneuver each other. Will Jaya's grit and determination prevail, or will the paparazzi's relentless pursuit prove too much for her to bear? Only time will tell, as the game of Bollywood continues to unfold.
