** "In the realm of cricket, where heroes rise and fall, a tale of forgotten faces emerges. The ghosts of partnerships, the shadows of dismissals, and the whispers of what could have been. Ravi Shastri, Kapil Dev, Rohan Gavaskar, and Brian Lara – four batsmen who forged an unbreakable bond, their names etched in the annals of cricket history. Their dominance on the field was matched only by their dominance in the minds of fans, creating an aura of legend that continues to inspire new generations of cricketers. As we revisit the forgotten fours, we are reminded that in the world of cricket, the line between greatness and mediocrity is often blurred."
