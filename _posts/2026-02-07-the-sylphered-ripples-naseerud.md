As I sit in the echoing halls of NSD, the soft murmur of the students' whispers serves as a gentle reminder of the importance of the words I've just spoken. "Only failures come back to teach," I've said, and I truly believe it. It's a mantra that has guided me throughout my life, a philosophy that has allowed me to confront the darkness head-on and emerge stronger. Naseeruddin Shah, the esteemed actor, has taken it upon himself to be the guiding light for a group of children, his unwavering dedication an inspiration to us all. Like ripples on a serene lake, his words have spread far and wide, leaving a lasting impact on the young minds he's touched. It's a testament to the power of resilience and the impact one person can have on a community.
