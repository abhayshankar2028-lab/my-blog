**BODY:**
In the twilight of a cricketing era, where legends roamed free,
A moment in time beckons, a walk down memory lane to see.
The ghosts of greatness haunt the pavilions, the grandstands too,
Their whispers weave a tapestry of triumphs, of sweat and toil anew.

Like a master painter, Sir Don Bradman's strokes danced on the board,
A symphony of elegance, a dance of unyielding force and cord.
The spin of Malcolm Marshall, a mesmerizing spell, a wizard's art,
The curve of Shane Warne, a wicket-taker's lair, where batsmen fell apart.

The voices of Brian Lara, Vivian Richards, and Sachin Tendulkar resound,
Echoes of a golden era, where cricket's mighty hearts were bound.
Their stories etched in the annals of time, a testament to the game's might,
A moment in time, forever etched, in the hearts of cricket's greatest light.
