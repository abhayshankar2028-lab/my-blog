The delay, announced by Dhurandhar 2, had left the industry reeling, with directors of the film expressing their disgust. "How dare you have your own successful film industry?" they questioned, as if the very concept of India's cinematic prowess was a affront to their global dominance. The timing was not coincidental; the film's success had already begun to make waves in the international market, leaving Hollywood's directors to wonder if their own industry was being left in the dust. As the debate rages on, it remains to be seen whether India's borrowed formula will be enough to keep the Hollywood giants at bay.
