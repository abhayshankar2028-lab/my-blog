As the sun sets over the majestic city of Udaipur, a sinister tale unfolds, shrouded in the shadows of deceit and betrayal. Shwetambari Bhatt, the wife of the renowned filmmaker Vikram Bhatt, stands accused of embezzling millions, leaving the city reeling in its wake. The whispers of her guilt echo through the streets, a haunting melody that refuses to be silenced. Her husband, once a pillar of the film industry, now finds himself imprisoned, a victim of his own mortal coil. The weight of the truth bears down upon him, a crushing burden that threatens to destroy the very fabric of their family's legacy. As the city holds its breath, the drama unfolds, a complex web of lies and deceit that will ultimately lead to the downfall of those who thought themselves above the law.
