As I stood at the edge of the bathtub, the warm water glistening in the dim light, I couldn't help but feel like a king, or in this case, a national hero. It was the story of how Mahesh Bhatt's mid-shower pitch won me – and many others – a coveted National Award. The way he effortlessly delivered those two words, "I'm taking a bath," was like a poetic monologue, a masterclass in understatement and charisma. It was as if he had conjured the entire set of the film on that single pitch. I recall thinking, "How does he do it?" And then, the floodgates opened, and the award was mine. It was a turning point in my career, a moment that catapulted me to stardom. Even now, I'm still on cloud nine, reliving the magic of that bath, and the impact it had on my life.
