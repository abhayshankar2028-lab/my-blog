The whispers of Hrithik Roshan, the man who once lit up the screen for her, now seemed a distant memory, a fleeting solace in a sea of sorrow.

The answer lay in the shadows, where the parasitic infection had left its mark, a testament to the cruel hand of time. Yet, even in the midst of darkness, Saba's spirit remained unbroken, a beacon of hope in a world that seemed determined to extinguish it. And so, as the cricketing world watched with bated breath, they waited with bated heart for the dawn of a new era, one where Saba Azad's resilience would be the benchmark for all to follow.
