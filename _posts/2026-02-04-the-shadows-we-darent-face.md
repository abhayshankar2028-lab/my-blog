As I delve into the world of Prakambanam, I'm met with a sense of unease, like a whispered secret in the dead of night. The film's protagonist, Ganapathi, navigates a labyrinthine path, where the lines between reality and paranoia blur. It's a horror comedy that laughs maniacally, a twisted dance of darkness and light. But beneath its veneer of humor, lies a scathing critique of the very politics that spawned it. Like a spider's web, the film ensnares its audience, pulling them into a world of suffocating dread. Will you dare to confront the truth, or succumb to the shadows that lurk within? Prakambanam is a cinematic experience that will leave you shivering, but also strangely exhilarated.
