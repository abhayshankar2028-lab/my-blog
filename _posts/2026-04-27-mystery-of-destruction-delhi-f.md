In a shocking turn of events, Delhi's batting lineup crumbled to a mere 40/6 in the powerplay, leaving them reeling and on the brink of collapse. The spin duo of Ravichandran Ashwin and Yuzvendra Chahal, once touted as the saviors of the team, failed to deliver, as Hazlewood and Bhuvneshwar continued their impressive form. The duo's relentless pressure and unyielding support proved too much for the Delhi batsmen, leaving them in disarray. Meanwhile, Kohli, the usually stalwart captain, emerged as a hero, his batting form a testament to his unwavering dedication. As the final wicket fell, Kohli took to the field, a masterclass in leadership, and with it, a reminder that even in the darkest of times, there is always a glimmer of hope.
