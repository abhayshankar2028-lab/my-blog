As he walked the corridors of history, the echoes of his greatness lingered, a testament to the timeless power of cricket.

In this walk down memory lane, I invite you to join me on a journey through the ages, where the ghosts of greatness roam free. Let us relive the moment that defined a generation, where Tendulkar's legend was born, and the world was forever changed by the magic of cricket.
