Imagine a game of cricket, where the stakes are high and the tension is palpable. But when a sequel arrives, it's like watching a perfectly played game of whist, with all the excitement and suspense stripped away.

"Ready or Not 2" promises to deliver, with a fresh cast and a new villain, but what it brings is a sequel that runs in circles, playing the same old tricks and relying on tired tropes. The plot, like a dull, overcooked curry, is predictable and lacks the depth and complexity that previous installments had. The acting, too, is a disappointment, with the leads struggling to elevate the material. It's a movie that promises much but delivers nothing new, leaving audiences feeling underwhelmed and unsatisfied. Will it be the start of a long, arduous journey, or a flash in the pan? Only time will tell, but one thing is certain – this sequel is a mystery waiting to be solved, but unfortunately, it's not one worth solving.
