Like a symphony orchestrated by the gods, the songs that will haunt our dreams, and the silences that will shatter our hearts. Syndicate is a symphony of shadows, a requiem for the universe, and a tribute to Dhurandhar 2, a masterwork that will forever be etched in our collective imagination.
