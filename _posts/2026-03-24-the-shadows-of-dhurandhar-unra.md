In the stifling heat of Dhurandhar, a web of deceit weaves its way through the lives of a select few. But the ultimate question remains: what fuels the fire that drives men to commit unspeakable acts?

As the story unfolds, the lines between truth and fiction blur, and the truth begins to unravel. Desperate to stay alive, the protagonist turns to the shadows, where the only certainty is the risk of exposure.
